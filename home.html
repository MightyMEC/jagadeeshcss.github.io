About us

Tap academy is an Ed-Tech company that helps an individual build their technical skills in the Information and technology industry. We provide excellent training, coaching, and mentoring to our students that enrich mould, and polish our students into having the most up-to-date technical skills, soft skills and qualities essential to the professional world. We have very carefully sculpted the courses keeping in mind the needs of the software industry and the best-suited method that could help the individual's learning experience be effortless and efficient. We specialise in teaching Java, Python, Full stack developer Java/Python, Data Science, Data Structure, Artificial Intelligence, some of the most trending languages in the industry. In the words of Frank Herbert "Actual learning requires for you to do those things" we believe that our students must engage in practical usage of the skills imparted thus we use a flip-class module of education where the theory of a concept is separately taught to our students through pre-recorded classes with visuals and animations along with study material to facilitate the learning on our LMS( learning management system), finishing off with a live class where students can join the trainer in practical coding. On completion of our course, we facilitate our student's transition into the professional world by providing them with opportunities by partnering with companies in the industry looking for the best candidates in the market. We bridge the gap between 90% of Engineering Universities generating underskilled graduates and the need for adept and versed manpower by the Tech-Industry.
